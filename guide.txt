Haridd - Quick Guide
====================

Controls:
- Move: A/D
- Jump: Space or K
- Dash: Left Shift or J
- Attack: L or Left Mouse
- Up/Down + Attack for directional slashes (Down = Pogo)
- Pause: Esc
- Inventory: I
- Consumables: 4/5/6 keys
- Skills: 1/2/3 keys (class-specific)

Developer Cheats:
- F1  God Mode (toggle)
- F2  Refill Consumables
- F3  Toggle Enemy Vision Rays
- F4  Open Debugger Menu
- F5  Open Debugger Menu (alternative)
- F6–F10  Jump to Rooms 1–5

ASCII Map Legend:
- # wall/platform
- . empty
- S spawn
- D door to next room
- E Bug, f Frog, r Archer, w Wizard Caster, a Assassin, b Bee, B Boss, G Golem (boss)

Classes:
- Knight: Tanky melee with Shield/Power/Charge skills
- Ranger: Ranged with charge shots, triple-shot, and speed boost
- Wizard: Spellcaster with Fireball, Cold Field, and Magic Missiles

Inventory System:
- Press I to open inventory overlay
- Manage consumables and armaments
- Default consumables vary by class:
  - Knight: Health Flask, Cavern Brew, Mana Vial
  - Ranger: Health Flask, Skyroot Elixir, Haste Draught
  - Wizard: Mana Vial, Skyroot Elixir, Cavern Brew

Monsters (color = rectangle fill color; darker variant indicates i-frames):
- Bug (E)
  - Color: RGB(180,70,160) violet; i-frames: RGB(120,40,100)
  - Smart ground enemy: patrol/search/pursue with vision cone, contact damage, affected by slow/DOT, parry reflects.

- Frog (f)
  - Color: RGB(80,200,80) green; i-frames: RGB(60,120,60)
  - Shows "!" then longer diagonal dash toward player; touch damage.

- Archer (r)
  - Color: RGB(200,200,80) yellow; i-frames: RGB(120,120,60)
  - "!!" telegraph then arrow (speed ~10, lifetime ~120). Sidesteps if too close.

- Wizard Caster (w)
  - Color: RGB(180,120,220) lavender; i-frames: RGB(110,80,140)
  - "!!" telegraph; casts Bolt (fast), Missile (very fast, high dmg), Fireball (slow, AoE ~48).

- Assassin (a)
  - Color: RGB(60,60,80) dark slate; i-frames: RGB(40,40,60)
  - Semi-invisible: Randomly chooses "!" diagonal dash (spawns sword hitboxes) or "!!" forward slash.

- Bee (b)
  - Color: RGB(240,180,60) amber; i-frames: RGB(140,120,50)
  - Random "!" dash or "!!" ranged shot (visible arrow-like projectile).

- Boss (B)
  - Color: RGB(200,100,40) orange-brown; i-frames: RGB(140,80,30)
  - Higher HP, slow pursuit, strong contact damage; doors remain locked in boss rooms until defeated.

- Golem (G)
  - Color: RGB(140,140,160) steel; i-frames: RGB(100,100,120)
  - Patterns: "!" diagonal dash, "!!" shoot, "!!" radial stun AoE (applies stun tag, radius ~72).

Debugger Menu:
- Toggle God Mode, Infinite Mana, Zero Cooldown
- Teleport to any room
- Give items and equipment
- Refill consumables
- Toggle enemy vision rays
